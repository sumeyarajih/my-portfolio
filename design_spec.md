# Portfolio Design Specification
**Client:** Sumeya Rajih Abass  
**Role:** Software Engineer (Flutter, UI/UX) & Full Stack Developer  
**Vibe:** Clean, Bold, Trustworthy  

## 1. Design Principles & Aesthetics

### Core Philosophy
*   **Clean:** Abundant whitespace, clutter-free interfaces, and precise alignment. The design should breathe.
*   **Bold:** High-contrast typography and strategic use of the **Orange** accent color to draw attention to call-to-actions (CTAs) and key skills.
*   **Trustworthy:** Professional typography, consistent spacing, and clear, accessible content presentation. High-quality visuals of work.

### Color Palette
A high-contrast, modern palette.
*   **Primary Background:** `#FFFFFF` (White) - For the main canvas, keeping it clean.
*   **Primary Text:** `#1A1A1A` (Almost Black) - For headings and body text to ensure readability but softer than pure black.
*   **Accent / Brand Color:** `#FF6B00` (Vibrant Orange) or `#FD7E14` - Used for primary buttons, active states, and highlighting key words/graphics.
*   **Secondary Elements:** `#F5F5F5` (Light Gray) - For subtle section backgrounds or card borders.
*   **Clean Black:** `#000000` - For boldest headers or primary buttons (if not orange).

**Usage Rule:** 
*   Backgrounds are predominantly white.
*   Text is black/dark grey.
*   **Orange** is used sparingly but effectively (e.g., the "Hire Me" button, skill progress bars, or hover effects).

### Typography
*   **Headings:** *Space Grotesk*, *Outfit*, or *Syne*. (Bold, modern sans-serifs with personality).
*   **Body:** *Inter*, *DM Sans*, or *Plus Jakarta Sans*. (Highly readable, clean, modern).
